## What happened
- 2026-05-13: Issue opened reporting that the `pr-description` recipe hung indefinitely on the `qwen3.6:35b-a3b-q8_0` model with a ~5KB input, pegging CPU at 100% and requiring manual kill after 8 minutes.
- 2026-05-13T09:14:22Z: user-a confirmed repro on the same model with a 4.2KB stat input, noting `ollama ps` showed the request queued at 100% with no token output, while tiny prompts returned in 1.2s.
- 2026-05-13T11:46:01Z: user-b bisected the issue, finding the recipe stalls past ~3KB of prompt input on this hardware, and filed an upstream bug against ollama.
- 2026-05-13T14:02:35Z: maintainer confirmed the issue and announced a plan to add a pre-flight canary to `delegate.sh` to prevent callers from sinking input collection effort.

## What's blocking
- The `pr-description` recipe stalls indefinitely on inputs above ~3KB on the `qwen3.6:35b-a3b-q8_0` model, causing 100% CPU usage and no token output.

## What's next
- maintainer will add a pre-flight canary to `delegate.sh` using a 1-token probe with a bounded timeout (`DELEGATE_PREFLIGHT_TIMEOUT=10`) to exit 3 with recovery options if the canary times out.
